About

Our Vision

To create a research centred platform that turns learning into meaningful action by enabling young researchers to apply existing skills in research and development, explore contemporary geospatial tools, and gain practical experience that connects academic knowledge with real-world industry needs.

Our Mission

Establish a group of researchers to strengthen quality of research by integrating cutting-edge geospatial technologies and data for smart and sustainable development.

Key Areas

Key focus areas guiding our research and development activities

Smart Sensing & Earth Observation

Develop advanced sensor networks for real-time climate parameter monitoring including temperature, humidity, air quality, and weather patterns.

Spatial Analytics & Geo-AI

Create environmentally friendly sensing technologies that contribute to sustainable development goals and climate change mitigation efforts.

Big Data & Cloud Computing

Implement AI and machine learning algorithms to analyze sensor data and provide actionable insights for decision-making processes.
